Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: July 1, 2023

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request the deletion of your personal data from Instagram Auto Uploader by following these steps:

  1. Send an email to your-email@example.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
  2. In your email, please include:
    • The email address associated with your Instagram Auto Uploader account
    • Your full name
    • Confirmation that you wish to delete all your personal data

We will process your request and delete your personal data within 14 days of receiving your request. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon your request, we will delete:

  • Your account information
  • Your Instagram account connections
  • Your uploaded videos and content
  • Any scheduled posts
  • Usage history and preferences

Please note that data that has already been posted to Instagram through our service cannot be automatically deleted from Instagram's servers.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the data deletion process, please contact us at your-email@example.com.
