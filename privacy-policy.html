Privacy Policy for Instagram Auto Uploader

Last Updated: July 1, 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to Instagram Auto Uploader. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information: email address, name
  • Instagram Business account information: account credentials, access tokens
  • Content: Videos and captions you upload for scheduling to Instagram
  • Usage data: How you interact with our application

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To schedule and post content to your Instagram Business accounts
  • To notify you about changes to our service
  • To provide customer support
  • To improve our application

4. Data Storage and Transfer

Your videos are stored in Cloudflare R2 storage. Post schedules and account information are stored in Firebase. All data is transmitted securely using encryption.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Instagram/Meta: To publish content on your behalf
  • Service providers: Cloudflare and Firebase for storage and processing
  • Legal requirements: When required by law

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data
  • Withdraw consent
  • Object to processing of your information

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under 16 years of age.
